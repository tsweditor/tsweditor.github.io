Read Cooked Content

The editor allows for reading of existing files that are shipped with TSW.

Based on UE4 Editor

The editor is simply a modification of the standard UE4 editor. With additional types of assets such as formations, and some tweaks to allow for cooked content in editor.

Still in development...

The tools aren't at a stable point yet and are still being worked on, so to begin with it is limited to only editing of data assets.
